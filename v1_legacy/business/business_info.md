## Frequently asked questions

1. **Do you accept walk-ins?** / តើអាចមកដោយមិនបានណាត់ជួបទុកមុនបានទេ?
   Yes, walk-ins are welcome, but booking an appointment first is recommended,
   especially on weekends.

2. **Is treatment painful?** / តើការព្យាបាលឈឺទេ?
   We use local anesthesia for most procedures, so patients feel little to no
   pain during treatment.

3. **Do you treat children?** / តើគ្លីនិកព្យាបាលកុមារដែរឬទេ?
   Yes, we treat children from age 5 and up.

4. **What payment methods do you accept?** / តើទទួលទូទាត់ប្រាក់តាមមធ្យោបាយអ្វីខ្លះ?
   Cash, ABA Pay, and Wing.

5. **Do you accept health insurance?** / តើទទួលធានារ៉ាប់រងសុខភាពដែរឬទេ?
   Not at the moment — we currently accept cash and mobile payment only.

6. **Is parking available?** / តើមានកន្លែងចតឡាន/ម៉ូតូដែរឬទេ?
   Yes, free parking for cars and motorbikes is available in front of the clinic.
